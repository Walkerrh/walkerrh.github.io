People come together as a response to their own weaknesses. If one was strong enough to provide for themselves then they would seek peace, stability, and security in isolation. The question of how to collectively operate within a large group arises. In seeking how to create the same peace, stability, and security from inside the large group, which is naturally chaotic, decision making must be narrowed, and the priority of the group is placed above the individual.
