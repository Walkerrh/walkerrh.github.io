# Hot Pot Overview

## Ingredients
- Base Broth (chicken, beef, or vegetable)
- Cooking Oil
- Aromatics (garlic, ginger)
- Eggs
- Thinly sliced meat (beef, pork, chicken)
- Seafood (shrimp, fish)
- Vegetables (bok choy, spinach, mushrooms)
- Tofu
- Noodles

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in the pot and sauté garlic and ginger until fragrant.
2. Crack and stir-fry the eggs in the hot oil.
3. Add the base broth to the pot and bring to a simmer.
4. Add thinly sliced meats, seafood, vegetables, tofu, and noodles.
5. Allow ingredients to cook in the simmering broth.
6. Serve hot with dipping sauces.

## Dipping Sauces
- Soy sauce with garlic and chili
- Sesame paste with soy sauce and vinegar
- Ponzu sauce

# Hot Pot Recipe

## Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
- 4 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 8 cups of base broth (chicken, beef, or vegetable)
- 8 ounces of thinly sliced meat (beef, pork, chicken)
- 8 ounces of seafood (shrimp, fish)
- 4 cups of assorted vegetables (bok choy, spinach, mushrooms)
- 8 ounces of tofu, cubed
- 4 ounces of noodles (rice noodles, udon, or egg noodles)

## Instructions
1. **Heat Oil and Sauté Aromatics**:
   - Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil in the pot over medium heat.
   - Add 4 cloves of minced garlic and 1-inch piece of sliced ginger.
   - Sauté for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant.

2. **Add and Cook Eggs**:
   - Pour 2 beaten eggs into the pot.
   - Stir continuously for about 1 minute until the eggs are scrambled and cooked through.

3. **Add Broth**:
   - Add 8 cups of base broth to the pot.
   - Bring the broth to a simmer over medium-high heat.

4. **Add Meats and Seafood**:
   - Add thinly sliced meat and seafood to the simmering broth.
   - Cook the meat for about 3-5 minutes until it changes color and is cooked through.
   - Cook the seafood for about 2-4 minutes until it is opaque and cooked through.

5. **Add Vegetables and Tofu**:
   - Add 4 cups of assorted vegetables and 8 ounces of cubed tofu to the pot.
   - Simmer for about 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender.

6. **Add Noodles**:
   - Add 4 ounces of noodles to the pot.
   - Cook for about 3-5 minutes until the noodles are tender and cooked through.

7. **Serve**:
   - Serve hot, allowing diners to fish out cooked ingredients and dip them in various sauces.

## Dipping Sauces
- **Soy Sauce Mix**:
  - 1/4 cup soy sauce
  - 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  - 1 teaspoon chili flakes
- **Sesame Paste Mix**:
  - 2 tablespoons sesame paste
  - 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  - 1 teaspoon vinegar
- **Ponzu Sauce**:
  - 1/4 cup ponzu sauce

### Notes
- Adjust the heat as needed to maintain a simmer, not a rolling boil.
- Ensure that all ingredients are fresh and properly prepared.
- Customize the ingredients and dipping sauces to your preference.

Enjoy your Hot Pot meal with friends and family!



# Electric Hot Pot

## Features
- Built-in heating element
- Adjustable temperature control
- Removable pot for easy cleaning
- Handles for safe transport

## Usage
1. Place the electric hot pot at the center of the table.
2. Fill with the broth and ingredients as per the recipe.
3. Plug in and set to the desired temperature.
4. Adjust heat as necessary to maintain a simmer.
5. Enjoy cooking and dining together.

# Donabe Pot (Japanese Earthenware)

## Features
- Made from clay, providing excellent heat retention.
- Traditionally used for Japanese-style hot pot dishes.

## Usage
1. Place the donabe on a portable gas or butane stove.
2. Fill with the broth and ingredients as per the recipe.
3. Set the stove to medium heat to bring the broth to a simmer.
4. Adjust heat as necessary to maintain a simmer.
5. Enjoy cooking and dining together.

### Example Brands
- Iga Mono
- Mino Ware
- Banko Ware

### General Tips
- Ensure the pot is large enough to hold all ingredients without overflowing.
- Use a portable heat source like a butane stove for table-side cooking.
- Keep a ladle and slotted spoon handy for serving and fishing out ingredients.
